About FitLayer

Making digital fashion shopping feel more certain .

FitLayer exists to close the gap between seeing a garment online and understanding how it may look on the person buying it.

Our story

A better product page should answer the shopper's biggest question.

Fashion commerce became faster, but the product experience remained static. FitLayer was created to turn product imagery into a personal, interactive decision tool.

M

Mission

Give every shopper a clearer, more personal understanding of the products they are considering.

V

Vision

Make interactive, AI-assisted product visualization a standard part of fashion commerce.

F

Future

Move beyond static catalogs toward adaptive storefronts that respond to each shopper.

Values that guide the product.

FitLayer is designed around trust, restraint, quality, and measurable usefulness.

Useful before impressive

Technology should reduce uncertainty, not add spectacle without purpose.

Privacy by design

Personal imagery deserves explicit controls and responsible handling.

Built for real commerce

Every feature must work within the operational reality of retailers.
